Tip of the Month: The Power of a 5-Minute Break

Feeling overwhelmed? Don't underestimate the power of a short break. Taking just five minutes to step away from your work can significantly reduce stress. Try one of these quick activities:

  • Practice deep breathing exercises.
  • Stretch your body or take a short walk.
  • Listen to a calming song.
  • Simply close your eyes and rest.
